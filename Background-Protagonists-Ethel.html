Bass Corvana: Colossal Warrior
Background: Bass was a child sold on the Chinese Market by a rich, sterile US family that adopted him because he was a rarity. He was seen more as a trophy than their child, so once old enough he stole his father's boat and set sail lost at sea in the Eastern Hemisphere. Bass' parents lived in the rich suburbs of Rhode Island. The neighbors were snobbish, but they were decent people.
Bass' father liked to take him in boat trips and teach him how to fish, so he knows how to pilot a boat. He liked his parents, but they don't really like him or so to speak. As mentioned before, he felt like a trophy or a decorative doll rather than a son. They want to mold his character the way they want their son to be. They wanted him to become a lawyer (which is why he was able to debunk Vizer's claim in old skit), giving him solid comprehensive reading. They even want him to be a musician (he plays Brass instruments). There really wasn't many freedom for him in the household. However, the few things he did enjoy there was that his dad taught him on how to wrestle and box. He taught him how to fish, read a map, and drive a boat. He loves those things. They let him read/watch eastern martial arts novels/videos which he became a fan of.
